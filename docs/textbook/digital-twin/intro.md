# Weeks 6-7: Digital Twin Workflows

This section covers digital twin concepts and workflows - virtual replicas of physical systems used for simulation, testing, and optimization. You'll learn how to create and use digital twins for robotics development and testing.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this section, you will:
- Understand digital twin concepts and their applications in robotics
- Create virtual replicas of physical robotic systems
- Implement simulation workflows for testing and validation
- Use digital twins for system optimization
- Connect digital twins with real-world systems

## Topics Covered

- Digital twin fundamentals
- Simulation environments
- Physics engines for robotics
- Real-time synchronization
- Testing and validation workflows
- Digital twin applications in robotics

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of basic robotics concepts
- Familiarity with simulation tools
