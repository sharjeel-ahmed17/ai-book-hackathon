# Weeks 11-13: VLA Models and Humanoid Robotics

This section explores Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models and their applications in humanoid robotics. You'll learn how to implement and use VLA models for complex robotic tasks and human-robot interaction.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this section, you will:
- Understand Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models
- Implement VLA models for robotics applications
- Apply VLA models to humanoid robotics tasks
- Understand the challenges and opportunities in humanoid robotics
- Integrate perception, planning, and control for humanoid systems

## Topics Covered

- Vision-Language-Action models overview
- VLA for robotics applications
- Humanoid robotics challenges
- Manipulation and locomotion
- Human-robot interaction
- Advanced humanoid control systems

## Prerequisites

- Deep learning knowledge
- Understanding of robotics control
- Familiarity with perception systems
